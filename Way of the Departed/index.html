Way of the Departed is a "could-be-canon" Ninjago novel written by show co-creator Tommy Andreasen and released on Twitter. It takes place between The Hands of Time and Sons of Garmadon, and focuses on Cole's journey to discover the origins of his scar.

Story and illustrations by Tommy Andreasen: @TommyAndreasen
